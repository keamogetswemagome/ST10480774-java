Our Story

Sasko’s Kitchen began with a simple dream: to bring family-style meals to the community.
What started as a small family tradition has now grown into a beloved restaurant known for
its fresh, homemade dishes and warm hospitality. Sasko (Pty) Ltd is a prominent South
African food manufacturer from vosloorus specializing in delicious African cuisines.
Established in 2023, the company operates 3 Kitchens nationwide, delivering over a thousands
hearts daily families. Sasko is renowned for its diverse range of products, including wrap
kotas, pasta, local foods and other goods, catering to both retail and industrial.
